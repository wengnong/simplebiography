Profile


Zhang Ling He is a Chinese actor born in Wuxi, Jiangsu, China. He was admitted to Nanjing Normal University in 2016, majoring in Electrical Engineering in the Faculty of Electrical and Automation Engineering. During this time, a chance meeting with an agent led him to discover a significant interest in acting, which steered him to pursue entering the show business circle. He debuted as an actor in the 2020 series, 'Maiden Holmes'. On January 20, 2021, he won the "New Force of the Year" award at the 5th Jinguduo Network Film and Television Festival.
